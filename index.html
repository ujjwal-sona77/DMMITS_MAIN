What is Generative AI?

Generative AI (GenAI) is a branch of artificial intelligence that can create new content, ideas, and solutions. It powers everything from text and image generation to music, code, and even scientific discovery.

How it Works

GenAI uses neural networks and deep learning to understand patterns and generate new data that mimics human creativity.

Why it Matters

It enables automation, personalization, and innovation at a scale never seen before, transforming industries and daily life.
